style guide:

Aestheticand Layout: For this I wanted to keep it very minimal and simple. I did not want to do too much as that can overpower the website and make it much difficult for the viewer to engage along with feeling drawn to the website.


Font: The font I use throughout my entire website was ‘Lara’ font. I love how minimal it is, however, I am open to expanding on it by using other fonts for other headings, but only if I find one that is visually appealing to my website and me.


Colour Scheme: As seen from most of the information along with digital art interactions displayed the colour scheme for this is shades of grey. The reason as to why I used shades of grey is to represent the asteroids and their colour, another reason being, I want my data visualisations and art to match the colour scheme of my website, however I need to first structure my data out and figure the colour scheme I intend to use there before I decide on one.
